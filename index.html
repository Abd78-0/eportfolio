About Me

Hello — I'm Abdul Hamid. I am a Year 2 Mechatronics student at Temasek Polytechnic. This page documents my journey of guided learning — the reflections I’ve made and the projects I’ve worked on along the way.

About Guided Learning

Guided Learning is a TPFun subject that encourages students to take initiative in exploring new areas of knowledge. It provides the freedom to pick up new skills, dive into unfamiliar subjects, or even work on real‑world projects.

For my Guided Learning project, I chose to study full‑stack development through Harvard’s CS50W course. As an engineering student, I am passionate about problem‑solving and understanding the building blocks that power systems “under the hood.” Beyond that, I aspire to develop IoT applications, such as smart device control, where hardware and software meet to create practical solutions.
